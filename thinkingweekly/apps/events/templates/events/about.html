About Thinking Liverpool

Interesting events in one place

Since moving to the city in 2013, I've been amazed by the number of talks, debates and other interesting events in Liverpool.

The problem was there seemed to be a hundred different places to look, and I kept hearing about things after they'd already happened.

Connecting curious people

I started Thinking Liverpool to make it easier to find out about events and to bring together curious people from different walks of life.

Take a look at past updates then please go ahead and subscribe to my weekly email.

Cheers,

Paul
